Compliance with Legal Requirements

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

We adhere to GDPR principles to ensure lawful, fair, and transparent processing of personal data. GDPR compliance includes:

  • Data minimization and purpose limitation
  • Ensuring accuracy and security of personal data
  • Providing mechanisms for users to exercise their rights
Data Protection Act (DPA)

The DPA complements GDPR in the UK. We ensure that data is:

  • Processed lawfully and transparently
  • Collected for specific purposes
  • Kept secure and not retained longer than necessary

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Transfer your data to another service provider
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of specific data processing activities
